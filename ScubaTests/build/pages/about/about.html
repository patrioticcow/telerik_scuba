About "Scuba Test"

Scuba Test is a collections or questions to get your ready for the final test or just to refresh your memory.


How to

To begin simply tap on a question group. You will be provided with a set or questions and answers. Tap on a option button to verify if your answer is correct. You can slide to the left to skip a question. To clear all stats simply tap the "clear" button.
